Adding Pupils to your BounceTogether platform

In this article we describe how you can add pupils to Bounce

Navigation
Posted 03/09/2018 @Bob Wilkinson Lesson time: 30 min

Introduction

Before your users can complete a survey, you must first add them to the platform. This article describes how this happens and details what you need to do.

Objectives

  • Understand the methods you can use to add users to Bounce
  • know how users are added and what's involved in the process
  • Be ready for launching Bounce to your users after they are added

Let's get started!

Preparation

The BounceTogether platform is flexible and enables you to add pupils to your platform in a few different ways. The most convenient way by far is using an automatic tool, provided by either GroupCall or Wonde that automatically provision pupils to the BounceTogether platform as they are added to your management information system. There are alternatives if your Management System isn't supported but don't worry - it probably is.

No matter what version of Bounce you ordered, an automatic provisioning tool is included FREE OF CHARGE and it always will be.

Depending on what you chose when you ordered BounceTogether, a member of the Groupcall team will contact you to arrange the installation and can guide you through the remainder of this process. They are experts at setting this up on your behalf so all you really need to do here is arrange a date/time for them to do so. They may have already emailed you with the next steps.

You could opt to install the Groupcall software yourself but we recommend you speak to your IT contact to decide what the best option is if you are unsure. The Bounce team are always here to help if you need anything.

Groupcall will also keep your users fully up-to-date if they change class and when their details change in your School Management System. Here's a diagram showing how it works...

Authorising Bounce

Usually, if you are already using one of these providers for other applications in your School (they are the most popular solutions on the market!) then the installation will be quick and simple. Once installed, you will need to authorise BounceTogether for use.

Authorisation is an important step in the process as it enables you to grant permission for GroupCall's Xporter, to send data to BounceTogether. This allows you to restrict the classes that get distributed to the platform if you wish.

It is easy to authorise the BounceTogether platform to work with Groupcall's Xporter-on-demand.

  • In the initial email from Groupcall, you should have received a link inviting you to authorise bouncetogether
  • Click the link in this email to proceed. If you haven't received this link, click here instead
  • log in to GroupCall's Xporter-On-Demand console and follow the on-screen steps to authorise

Once the Bounce platform has received the authorisation as above, the users will be imported. This usually occurs within 24 hours of you authorising the application.

User Logins

When pupils have been imported into Bounce, you are ready to launch it to your users. The user logins are currently predetermined and allocated automatically by the Bounce platform. This means that when your users are imported, they will be automatically be assigned a username and password. These will be distributed to you by the Bounce team after your users are configured. We also issue you with a set of login cards enabling you to print and hand them out to your users.
