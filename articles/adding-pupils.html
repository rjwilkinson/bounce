Adding Pupils to your BounceTogether platform

In this article we describe the methods you can use to add pupils to Bounce.

Navigation
Posted 03/09/2018 @Bob Wilkinson

Introduction

In this article we'll walk you through a couple of ways that you can add pupils to your BounceTogether platform. Before you can create surveys, complete them or view data in the Dashboard showing the results, you will need to add pupils to the platform.

Automatic Provisioning

The Bounce platform is flexible and enables you to add pupils to your platform in a few different ways. The most convenient way by far is using an automatic tool, provided by either GroupCall or Wonde that automatically provision pupils to the Bounce platform as they are added to your management information system but there are alternatives if your Management System isn't supported (don't worry - it probably is).

When you ordered Bounce, if you indicated that your management system is one that is supported by the Bounce integrator (Groupcall or Wonde) then we will have automatically started provisioning your account with this provider. This means that a representative from one of these providers will be in contact with you to arrange the installation and can guide you through the remainder of the process. You may have already received the email to initiate this - it will have come from Groupcall.co.uk or Wonde.com so be sure to look out for it. You could opt to install the software yourself but we recommend you speak to your IT contact to decide what the best option is. The team are always here to help if you need us!

Authorisation

Usually, if you are already using one of these providers for other applications in your School (they are the most popular solutions on the market!) then the installation will be quick and simple. In any case, once you have been informed by your Groupcall/Wonde representative that the software has been installed successfully, you will need to authorise the Bounce application for use.

Authorisation is an important step in the process as it enables you to grant permission for GroupCall/Wonde to send data to Bounce. This allows you to restrict the information that gets distributed to the platform if you wish. We'll recommend a standard set of fields to submit to Bounce but if you want to restrict this then you can do. However, if you choose to do so, functionality in the Bounce app could be limited. For example, should you choose not to send "pupil premium" data, you will not be able to report on that demographic from the Dashboard.

Manually Provisioning

If you would prefer to maintain your pupil data in your Bounce platform yourself, you can do this by using a CSV file. If you can prepare a CSV file from your School Management System (e.g. SIMS, Progresso, RM Integris etc) then you you can import this into Bounce straight away.

You will need to create a csv file that has (at minimum) the following fields:
