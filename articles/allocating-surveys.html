Allocating Surveys to your BounceTogether users

Find out how to issue a survey to a user

Navigation
Posted 15/09/2018 @Bob Wilkinson

Introduction

BounceTogether is shipped with a bunch of carefully chosen surveys, which means you can allocate surveys to your users straight away. In fact, BounceTogether allocates the default set of surveys to your pupils when your site is setup. This article explains how you can allocate subsequent surveys to your users.

Hint

You will have a varying number of surveys to choose from, depending on the package you have purchased but even the Core package includes some to get you started

All BounceTogether users can create bespoke surveys on any subject matter that's desired!

Allocating a Task

You can allocate a survey to the whole organisation, a class or a specific user by using Tasks. The instructions that follow explain how to do this.

  1. Log in to the main console as a member of staff or the administrative user.
  2. Click Tools / Add To-Do Task from the top menu and the allocate task menu will show
  3. Select the details of the organisation, class, user (or any combination) depending on how you wish to allocate the survey. For instance, to allocate a survey to Class 6A like in the screenshot below, select the class from the Class or Group menu.

    If you wanted to allocate to a specific pupil, you would select them from the Person drop down.

  4. Next, enter the survey you want to allocate from the Questionnaire drop down menu. You can allocate several surveys in one go, just re-select another survey from the list.
  5. Next, enter the Due Date to reflect when this should be completed by then give the task a Name in the corresponding field.

Assigning a Badge

You can also allocate a badge to a survey so the pupil is rewarded with it when they complete it.

  1. When you are allocating a survey, click Add Badge to associate it with a badge
  2. Select the badge you want to associate to the survey and click Done

Confirming Options

After you have chosen the desired options, you can then confirm that you wish to allocate the survey.

  1. Click Let's Get Started to view a summary of the options
  2. In the summary screen, check the allocation options are what you intended and click Yes
  3. The survey will be allocated in accordance with your selection and the users can take the survey when they are logged in.
