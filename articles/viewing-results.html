Viewing Survey Results on the BounceTogether platform

Learn about the many ways that you can compare results from completed surveys

Navigation
Posted 16/09/2018 @Bob Wilkinson

Introduction

BounceTogether has been designed to make it easy for you to view and compare the results of completed surveys. This article demonstrates how you can navigate the platform to view the results of a survey you have issued to a group of users. If you want to have even more oversight of the results and run comparisions between groups/classes, check out the Dashboard.

Logging in

Before you can view the results of a survey your users have taken, you must be logged in as a user that has permission to access the results of a survey. Only Staff or Admin level users can view this information for an entire class.

If you have forgotten your credentials, you may need to speak to your BounceTogether Administrator to gain access again.

Viewing Results

After you are logged in to the platform as an authorised user, click Home to make sure you are on the home-page of the console.

  1. To view the full results from a survey, click Survey / Survey Results from the top menu.
  2. The Survey Results screen will open, which is where you can start to apply a filter on the survey/pupil/class you want to see the results for.
  3. There are many choices you can make in the filter settings depending on what you want to see. For example, if you want to see the results of a survey spanning multiple groups/classes then select the survey name and leave the Class/Group box empty.
    4. Hint

    Press Space on the keyboard in the Questionnaire box to bring up a full list of the surveys like in the example below:

  4. If you want to see the results from a particular user then select it from under the Person filter.
  5. If you want to limit the results to between two dates then choose the desired values from the Between boxes.
  6. Click Display Result when you have chosen your filtering options and the results will display like in the example below.
  7. In the example above, all students are showing as there is nothing applied in the filter. To filter the results to a particular class select the class name from the Class / Group field and click Display Results to update.

View More Detail

Now that you know how to see the results from any survey, the next step will explain how to navigate the answers and get a breakdown of the answers by pupil.

  1. Ensure you are viewing the results of your chosen survey like in the example shown below
  2. The pie chart will show you an overall view of each question. To change to a different question in that survey you can use the Next and Back buttons.
  3. When you want to see the answers your users gave to each question, click the View Answers button. These results will show under the pie chart and you can scroll further down the page to see each pupil's answers.
    4. Hint

    Scroll to the bottom of the results and you will notice options to Export and Print them.
